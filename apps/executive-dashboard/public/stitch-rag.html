What are the current policies regarding holiday leave for the upcoming winter season, and what is the process for vehicle delivery during peak times?

auto_awesome Internal AI Policy Expert

Based on the Employee Handbook 2024, holiday leave for the winter season (Dec 15 - Jan 15) must be submitted through the portal at least 45 days in advance1. Please note that a "Blackout Period" applies to the final three days of the month to ensure adequate floor coverage for end-of-year sales targets.

Regarding vehicle delivery, our Vehicle Delivery SOP v3.2 outlines a streamlined process for peak periods:

  • All pre-delivery inspections (PDI) must be completed 24 hours prior to the scheduled handover2.
  • Delivery specialists must use the "Quick-Sign" digital document suite to reduce in-person processing time to under 20 minutes3.
  • During peak hours, the secondary delivery bay (Bay B) is strictly reserved for high-volume model handovers2.
