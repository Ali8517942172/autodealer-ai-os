# Project Overview

Project:
AutoDealer Cars Enterprise Platform


## Objective

Build a scalable automotive management platform for vehicle rental operations.


## Core Areas

- Fleet Management
- Vehicle Availability
- Customer CRM
- Rental Booking
- Contract Management
- Payment Tracking
- Maintenance Scheduling
- Analytics Dashboard
- AI Automation


## Business Rules

1. Every vehicle must have complete history.
2. Every booking must link to customer + vehicle.
3. Vehicle availability must update automatically.
4. Maintenance status affects booking availability.
5. All financial transactions must be auditable.
